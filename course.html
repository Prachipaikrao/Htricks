Introduction

[What is Hacking ?]

The purpose of hacking are first is just to make mischief. The second is to make a statement and the third is to make money.Hacking is not a crime. It’s a problem solving activityHacking is a core element of startup entrepreneurship. If you are building agile and launching lean, you are a hacker. Business leaders who want to bake entrepreneurship into their company’s operating system should start with hacking and make it a key ingredient in their innovation toolkit. Process of ethical hacking includes Reconnaissance is the first stage, hackers gather all the necessary information about the targetthe domain name and its history, IP addresses, employee information, phone numbers, etc. This stage is also called footprinting,Scanning is stage involves scanning each component of the target’s entire system for any vulnerability that can be exploited. The hackers try to figure out how they would break into the system,Gaining access ,Maintaining access is stage, hackers create a backdoor in the system by creating their own vulnerability and uploading it into the system so that they don’t lose access to it,Clearing tracks is a standard hacker protocol, white and black hat alike. In this stage, hackers remove all evidence of their unauthorised activity in the target system by deleting their history, log files, changing registry settings, etc., Reporting is the final stage in the process of ethical hackingone which differentiates it from illegal hacking. Hackers are just people with innovative spirits and an in-depth knowledge of technology. Therefore, do whatever you like as long as you didn't make other people suffered and devastated on your own action.It is my hope that the hackers of the future will move beyond their limitations and become hacktivists.
